The Konkan (MMR) and Pune divisions together hold roughly 79% of all registered development, and the top 3 districts alone account for 51% of supply — a highly concentrated market where micro-market selection makes or breaks a launch.

Active vs Lapsed — Why It Matters

ACTIVE
Extension obtained before expiry
  • Project remains compliant
  • Marketing & sales activities continue
  • Project account operations uninterrupted
  • Customer confidence maintained
LAPSED
Extension not obtained before expiry
  • New marketing & booking activities may be restricted
  • Registration of new sale agreements may be affected
  • Project bank account withdrawals may be restricted
  • May affect buyer confidence
1 in 4 registered projects (26.4%) has lapsed across Maharashtra, and projects take an average of 4 years to complete. Staying ahead of your completion date protects sales momentum, banking operations and buyer trust.
